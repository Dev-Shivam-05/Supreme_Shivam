Who I Am

A Developer Who
Never Stops Building

I'm Shivam Bhadoriya — a self-taught full-stack developer from India with a burning obsession for clean code, scalable architecture, and shipping real products that solve real problems.

My journey started with curiosity and has evolved into a 300+ day unbroken coding streak that I have zero intention of breaking. I don't code to fill a portfolio — I code because building things is the most powerful form of expression I know.

I specialize in the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js), but my toolkit extends to Python, Docker, Firebase, Redis, and whatever else the problem demands. I believe in understanding systems at a deep level — not just gluing APIs together.

When I'm not coding, I'm writing about backend architecture on my blog, contributing to open source, or mentoring other self-taught developers. The mission is simple: be undeniable.
